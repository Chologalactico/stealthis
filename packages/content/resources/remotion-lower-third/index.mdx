## Lower Third Name Bug

A classic broadcast lower-third graphic for identifying people on screen. Slides in from the left, holds for 2 seconds, then slides back out. Includes a colored accent bar, name, and job title.

### Composition specs

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1280 × 720 |
| FPS | 30 |
| Duration | 4 s (120 frames) |

### Timeline

| Time | Action |
|---|---|
| 0–0.5 s | Slide in from left |
| 0.5–3.5 s | Hold |
| 3.5–4 s | Slide out to left |

### Customization

Change `NAME`, `TITLE`, and `ACCENT_COLOR` at the top of the file.
