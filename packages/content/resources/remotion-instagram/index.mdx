## Instagram Story Template

A full-bleed vertical story composition (1080×1920) ready for Instagram. Includes a story progress bar that fills over the 15-second duration, an animated headline with a stagger reveal, body text, and a swipe-up CTA.

### Composition specs

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1080 × 1920 |
| FPS | 30 |
| Duration | 15 s (450 frames) |

### Layers (bottom → top)

1. Gradient background
2. Story progress bar
3. Account handle (top-left)
4. Headline — stagger word reveal
5. Subtitle — fade in
6. CTA button — slide up
