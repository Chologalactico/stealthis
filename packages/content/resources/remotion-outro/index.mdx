## YouTube Outro Animation

A 20-second end screen for YouTube videos. Shows a "Thanks for watching" headline, an animated subscribe button that pulses, and two placeholder cards for recommended video links.

### Composition specs

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1280 × 720 |
| FPS | 30 |
| Duration | 20 s (600 frames) |

### Sections

- **0–2 s** — "Thanks for watching" fades in
- **2–5 s** — Subscribe button scales in with spring
- **5–20 s** — End card placeholders slide in; subscribe button pulses

### Customization

Set `CHANNEL_NAME` and `BRAND_COLOR` at the top of the file.
