## LinkedIn Post Video

A 6-second video composition in the LinkedIn post format (1200×628). Animates the profile avatar sliding in from the left, name and headline appearing, post text fading in line by line, and a like/comment count ticking up.

### Composition specs

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1200 × 628 |
| FPS | 30 |
| Duration | 6 s (180 frames) |

### Customize

Set `AUTHOR_NAME`, `AUTHOR_HEADLINE`, `POST_TEXT`, and `BRAND_COLOR` at the top.
