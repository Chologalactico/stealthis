## Kanban Board

A sprint Kanban board with four columns and full HTML5 drag-and-drop. Cards display priority badges, tag pills, assignee avatars, and due dates. Drag cards to reorder within a column or move them across columns with animated drop-zone highlighting. Click the plus icon to add a card inline, or click any card to open a detail modal with description and comments.

### Features
- HTML5 drag-and-drop between all four columns (Backlog, In Progress, Review, Done)
- Card detail modal with description, comments, due date, and priority
- Inline card creation at the bottom of any column
- Priority badges: Critical, High, Medium, Low with color coding
- Assignee filter buttons and real-time search by card title
- Column card count badges update live after every move
- Drop-zone highlight on drag-over for clear visual feedback
