## Testimonial Video

A 6-second animated testimonial card for marketing videos and social ads. The quote text reveals word-by-word, the avatar slides in from the left, star icons fill one by one with a spring, and the author name fades up at the end.

### Composition specs

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1280 × 720 |
| FPS | 30 |
| Duration | 6 s (180 frames) |

### Timeline

| Time | Action |
|---|---|
| 0–0.5 s | Card fades in |
| 0.5–3 s | Quote words reveal sequentially |
| 3–3.5 s | Star rating fills 1→5 |
| 3.5–4 s | Avatar + name slide in |
| 4–6 s | Hold |

### Customization

Set `QUOTE`, `AUTHOR_NAME`, `AUTHOR_ROLE`, and `STAR_COUNT` constants.
