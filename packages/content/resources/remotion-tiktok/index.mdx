## TikTok Video Template

A 15-second TikTok-format composition (1080×1920) mimicking the in-app UI: right-side action icons (like, comment, share), username and caption at the bottom, and a progress bar at the bottom edge.

### Composition specs

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1080 × 1920 |
| FPS | 30 |
| Duration | 15 s (450 frames) |

### UI elements

- Progress bar (bottom)
- Username + caption (bottom-left)
- Action icons — like, comment, share (right)
- Caption words stagger in sequentially
